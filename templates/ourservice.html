Welcome to Maendeleo Bank

MAENDELEO BANK PLC was the first Tanzanian company and bank to be listed on the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange Plc (DSE) through the Enterprise Growth Market Window, and thus became a Public Limited Company (PLC). Since its inception in September 2013, Maendeleo Bank has focused on serving the micro, small, and medium enterprise markets in Dar es Salaam, with the underlying vision of building a bank that truly understands, listens, and strives to improve people's lives through innovative banking services. We are proud to be Tanzania's most profitable and rapidly growing microfinance bank, serving thousands of customers.
